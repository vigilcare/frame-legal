Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

Frame Video Studio is a desktop tool used to create and publish short-form videos for Nexoria.

Information We Collect

Frame Video Studio may store the following information locally on the user's computer:

  • App settings
  • Generated video files
  • Video titles and captions
  • Social media connection settings
  • Access tokens used to connect to supported publishing platforms

When connecting to platforms such as TikTok, Facebook, Instagram or YouTube, those services may provide account identifiers, profile information, publishing permissions and upload status information needed to connect the account and publish content.

How We Use Information

Information is used only to:

  • Create short-form videos
  • Preview generated videos
  • Publish videos to connected social media accounts
  • Display upload status and publishing history
  • Maintain app settings

We do not sell personal information.

Data Storage

Frame Video Studio is primarily a local desktop application. Generated files and settings are stored on the user's own computer unless the user chooses to upload content to a connected third-party platform.

Third-Party Services

If the user connects TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube or another platform, the app uses that platform's API only for the actions authorised by the user, such as account connection and video publishing.

Each third-party platform has its own privacy policy and terms.

Data Deletion

Users can disconnect social accounts from within the app and delete generated videos from their computer. Users may also revoke app access through the relevant third-party platform settings.
