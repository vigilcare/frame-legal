Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Frame Video Studio ("the App") is a personal desktop application for automated video creation and publishing. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to third parties. All configuration, including API keys and access tokens, is stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

The App connects to third-party APIs on your behalf, including TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, Anthropic, and ElevenLabs. Your use of those services is governed by their respective privacy policies. The App passes your credentials directly to those services and does not retain or log them.

Data Storage

All data generated by the App — including scripts, audio, images, and videos — is stored locally on your device. No user data is uploaded to any server operated by Frame Video Studio.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Contact

For any questions regarding this policy, please contact the app owner directly.
